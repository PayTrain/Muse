Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the app?

Answer:

To install, simply download the installer from our website by navigating to the Download Page and clicking the button for your operating system. Once downloaded, open the file and follow the on-screen instructions. The process should only take a few minutes, and you'll be guided step-by-step. If you run into any issues, please reach out to our support team via the contact form below.

 

Do I need any extra equipment to start making music?

Answer:

Not at all! You can begin creating music right away with just your computer. However, adding equipment like a MIDI keyboard or an audio interface can enhance your experience, but these are entirely optional for beginners.

 

How do I save and export my projects?

Answer:

Saving your project is easy—just click on the “Save” button in the top menu, or press Ctrl+S (Windows) or Cmd+S (Mac) on your keyboard. To export, go to File > Export, choose your preferred format, and follow the prompts. Your music will be ready to share or listen to offline!

 

Why can't I hear any sound when I play back my tracks?

Answer:

First, ensure your computer's audio is not muted, and check if your headphones or speakers are properly connected. If you're still having trouble, make sure that the audio settings are set to your preferred output device (e.g., headphones or speakers). You can adjust these settings under Preferences > Audio Settings.

 

Does Muse have any built-in sounds or samples?

Answer:

Yes! Muse includes a library of high-quality sounds, samples, and loops to help spark your creativity. You'll find these under the “Library” tab in the main menu. Just drag and drop any sound onto your timeline to use it in your project.

 

Is there a quick way to learn the basics?

Answer:

Absolutely! We have multiple quick start guides and tutorials on our blog page which cover essential steps to get you started in minutes! If you're more of a visual learner, we have plenty of video tutorials on our YouTube channel as well. Plus, there are in-app tips tailored for beginners.

 

Is there a subscription required?

Answer:

You can download and use Muse for free with access to all core features! We also offer optional add-ons for advanced features and sounds, which you can explore under “Upgrades” in the main menu.

 

What should I do if the app is running slowly or freezing?

Answer:

If you experience performance issues:

  • Try closing other programs to free up memory.
  • Check that your project isn't too complex; large projects can slow down performance.
  • If issues persist, visit our support resources or reach out to us directly through the contact form below.
