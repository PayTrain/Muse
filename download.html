Technical Requirements

Processor (CPU)

  • Minimum: Dual-Core Processor, 2.0 GHz or higher
  • Recommended: Quad-Core Processor, 3.0 GHz or higher

To run Muse effectively, we recommend at least a quad-core processor for smooth performance.

 

Memory (RAM)

  • Minimum: 4 GB
  • Recommended: 8 GB or more

For efficient operation, a minimum of 4 GB of RAM is required. For better performance, especially when working with multiple tracks, 8 GB or more is ideal.

 

Storage

  • Minimum: 500 MB free disk space for installation
  • Additional: At least 5 GB available for audio projects

Ensure you have enough storage for both the software and your audio files. An SSD is recommended for faster load times.

 

Graphics

  • Minimum: Integrated graphics (e.g., Intel HD Graphics)
  • Recommended: Dedicated graphics card with 2 GB VRAM

Description: Muse can run on integrated graphics, but for optimal performance, especially with video plugins, a dedicated GPU is recommended.

 

Internet Connection

  • A stable internet connection is essential for the initial download, updates, and accessing support materials.
